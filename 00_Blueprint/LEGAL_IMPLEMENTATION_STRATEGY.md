# Applify: Legal & Implementation Strategy (The Master Filter)

## 🏛️ 1. The Legal Framework: "Facts vs. Expression"
Our operational principle is that while specific literary expression is protected, the underlying **facts, ideas, and methods** are free for functional implementation.

*   **No Direct Quotes:** We never use verbatim sentences from source material.
*   **Method Extraction:** We strip the narrative and extract the logic (e.g., "Habit Stacking") to treat it as a functional "system."
*   **Transformative Use:** Applify is a **Utility Tool (SaaS)**, not a summary. We transform information into code-driven execution.

## 🛡️ 2. The Sanitization Filter
To ensure 100% legal originality and brand protection:

*   **The Multi-Source Shield:** Always synthesize from multiple experts in a field to create a unique "Master Protocol."
*   **The Language of Mastery:** We use original branding to prevent Trademark confusion (e.g., using "The Applify Feedback Loop" instead of trademarked terms).
*   **Utility Over Narrative:** Value is delivered through Progress Tracking, UI components, and Interactive Modals, not literary text.

## 🤖 3. AI Directives for Gemini (Protocol Implementation)
When generating content, code, or database schemas for Applify, I must:

1.  **Rephrase to SaaS Voice:** Convert all advice into professional, action-oriented SaaS terminology.
2.  **Data-First Architecture:** Turn advice into measurable variables (e.g., `streak_count`, `milestone_id`, `mastery_percentage`).
3.  **Domain-Based Metadata:** Refer to content by its scientific domain (e.g., "Behavioral Science") rather than specific book titles in technical metadata.
4.  **The "Sanitization" Check:** Before finalizing any protocol, run it through the "Facts vs. Expression" filter to ensure it is a functional tool, not a derivative work.
