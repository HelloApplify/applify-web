Instead of selling based on time (7/30/60 days), we sell **The Master Protocol**.

---

## 🏗️ The 4 Implementation Modules
1. **Module 1: The Identity Audit**
   - Goal: Transition from "Outcome Thinking" to "Identity Thinking."
   - Action: Define the "Type of Person" you want to be. Cast 3 votes.
   - Tracker: Identity Score +1.

2. **Module 2: Environment Optimization**
   - Goal: Remove friction and make cues obvious.
   - Action: The "Kitchen Sweep" and "Desk Audit."
   - Tracker: Environment Clarity Score.

3. **Module 3: Repetition Mastery (The 2-Minute Rule)**
   - Goal: Establish the "Art of Showing Up."
   - Action: Perform the tiny version of the habit 3 days in a row.
   - Tracker: Streak Multiplier.

4. **Module 4: Systems & Compounding**
   - Goal: Habit Stacking and Automation.
   - Action: Link new habit to an existing anchor habit.
   - Tracker: System Completion (Progress 100%).
