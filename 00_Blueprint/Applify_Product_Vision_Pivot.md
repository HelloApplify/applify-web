# Applify: Product Vision & Strategy Pivot

## Core Concept
Moving away from a fixed 30-day "call to action" template toward a modular, **"Duolingo-style" SaaS model** for behavioral science. 
It bridges the gap between educational content and real-world application ("Duolingo for Life Skills").

## The Library & Modules
- **Protocols over Lessons**: Sell "Operating Systems" (e.g., "14-Day Wealth Calibration") rather than static "Lessons" (e.g., "Money Management").
- **80/20 Rule**: 20% educational content, 80% interaction and gamified application.
- **Micro-Sprints**: Break large topics into Atomic Modules (e.g., Part 1: "The Subscription Purge", Part 2: "The 48-Hour Impulse Buffer"). Each is a 5-10 minute interactive sprint ending with a real-life task.

## Gamification Architecture
- **Proof of Action**: To unlock the next segment, the user must upload "proof" (photo, checked box, synced metric).
- **Streak of Application**: Streaks are not for just opening the app. Streaks are rewarded for *applying the lesson* in the real world within a specific timeframe (e.g. tracking "Morning Sunlight" within 30 min of sunrise).

## The Protocol Engine (Scientific Way)
- **Spaced Repetition & Push Interventions**: The SaaS acts as a "live coach". It will send push-interventions (e.g., "Are you starting your deep work block? Open the focus timer now.") so users don't forget the module after 3 days.

## Architectural Requirements
### 1. Slide-Based Experience
Avoid long walls of text.
- **Segment 1 (The Hook)**: Lottie animation / story / choose-your-own-adventure to explain the science.
- **Segment 2 (The Simulation)**: A game (e.g., a "Budget Slider" surviving random emergency events).
- **Segment 3 (The Integration)**: A digital canvas/dashboard where users apply logic to their actual life immediately.

### 2. The Dashboard (The SaaS Part)
- The "Protocol" teaches the system. The "Dashboard" is where users track progress daily.
- E.g., buying a "Money Management" protocol unlocks the "Wealth Tracker" tool in the dashboard.

## Validation Strategy (Web MVP)
- Build a **Landing Page** with a "Library" view showing 6-9 modules. 
- **Lead Magnets (3 Core Modules)**:
  1. The "Deep Work" Sprint (Productivity)
  2. The "Financial Health" Audit (Money Management)
  3. The "Sleep Engine" Setup (Health/Habits)
- Provide digital downloads & automations (Smart PDF/Notion templates, 5-day Check-in email sequences) to ensure the web experience translates to life application.
- **Goal**: Validate if users are interested and willing to pay ("Pre-Sale" test) before committing to mobile app development.
