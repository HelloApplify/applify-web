# 🛠️ Protocol: The Atomic Morning Start (Level 1)

**Duration**: 10 Minutes (The "Make it Easy" Phase)
**Goal**: Cast a vote for the identity of a "Highly Productive Professional."

## 🌅 The Sequence (Habit Stacking)

1.  **The Trigger**: After I pour my first cup of water/coffee...
2.  **The Action (2-Minute Rule)**: I will sit at my desk and open one single task for the day.
3.  **The Environment Design**: My phone will be in a different room (out of sight) until this sequence is complete.

## 📝 Implementation Intention
> "I will **write down my top priority** at **8:00 AM** in my **Home Office**."

## 🧱 The 4 Laws Check
- **Law 1 (Obvious)**: Water/Coffee is the physical cue.
- **Law 2 (Attractive)**: The "reward" is the taste of the coffee and the feeling of clarity.
- **Law 3 (Easy)**: The "2-Minute Rule" ensures you don't feel overwhelmed by the whole workday.
- **Law 4 (Satisfying)**: Check off the "Morning Start" in your tracker immediately.
