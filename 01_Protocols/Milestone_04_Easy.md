# 🦅 Milestone 4: Friction Reduction (Make it Easy)

> "Standardize before you optimize. You can't improve a habit that doesn't exist." — James Clear

## 🔬 The Science: The 2-Minute Rule
Most people fail because they try to reach the "Elite" version of a habit on Day 1. The 2-Minute Rule is about mastering the **Gateway Habit**. Once you start the right behavior, it is much easier to continue it.

---

## 🟢 Slow Track: The Atomic Scale-Down
**Duration: 2 Minutes | Focus: Consistency**

### 1. The Shrink
Take the habit you defined in Milestone 3. Scale it down until it takes less than 2 minutes.
- *Goal*: Read for 30 minutes ⮕ *Atomic*: Read one page.
- *Goal*: Do 50 pushups ⮕ *Atomic*: Do 1 pushup.

### 2. The Success Anchor
For the next 3 days, you are **only** required to do the Atomic version. If you do more, it's a bonus. But the 1-page/1-pushup is your "100% Success" mark.

### 3. Friction Reduction
Identify one tool or setting that makes the habit easier.
- *Example: Buying a better keyboard to make writing more enjoyable.*
- *Example: Joining a gym that is directly on your commute home (Zero detour).*
